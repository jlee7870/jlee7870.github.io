        In my free time, I also play the acooustic guitar and dance!

        ## Guitar

        I've been playing fingerstyle acoustic guitar for over 15 years and counting. I'm mostly self-taught, and learned from online YouTube tutorials, and whatever free music sheets I could find.

        I was originally inspired to play by guitarist Sungha Jung, and I even went to see some of his concerts when I was younger. I play less frequently nowadays, but my guitar still sits next to my desk whenever I feel inspired to play.

        ## Dance

        I've also been dancing for almost 10 years now, mostly dancing hip-hop and house. I started dancing because I enjoyed taking dance classes near where I grew up. I mostly went to "O School" and "Recognize! Studio", the latter being the birthplace of the Jam Republic agency which now houses world famous dancers like Sean Lew, Bada Lee, Rie Hata, and more!
        
        I've also had the privilege of learning house from the Versa-Style Dance Company at the Kaufman dance department at UCLA. In college, I joined dance teams and competed at venues like Prelude Norcal, Prelude Socal, Maxt Out, and more. I once had the goal of competing at the Vibe Dance Competition, but am finding increasingly little time for that.

        ## Other

        Other than that, I enjoy playing video games, and dabble in chess.

        My current chess goal is to play at an open tournament. My lifetime chess goal is to become a titled player.
